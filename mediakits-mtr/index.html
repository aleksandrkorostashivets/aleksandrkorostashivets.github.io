About MTR

Marine Technology Reporter is the world’s leading publication dedicated to underwater technology and ocean science. We cover autonomous systems, subsea robotics, sensors, imaging, communications, survey/hydrography, subsea energy, and defense. Our mission: connect innovators with buyers and practitioners who put technology to work.

Marine Technology Reporter + MarineTechnologyNews.com — the magazine, website, eNewsletter, digital edition, video/webcasts, and social channels—form the underwater technology community’s most trusted media portfolio. This integrated network ensures your message reaches engineers, operators, and researchers wherever they consume content.

As the flagship brand dedicated to underwater technology and ocean science, Marine Technology Reporter delivers independent analysis, breaking news, and essential market intelligence to subsea energy developers, defense programs, hydrographic/survey teams, research institutions, OEMs, and technology providers worldwide.

We provide a precise, cost-efficient media buy that connects your brand with the decision-makers who specify, fund, and deploy subsea systems. Advertisers leverage our authority, reach, and deep domain expertise to launch products, build demand, and lead their categories.

From AUV/ROV breakthroughs and sensors/imaging to communications, autonomy, power & batteries, survey/hydrography, and real-world project case studies, Marine Technology Reporter remains the essential resource for professionals navigating a rapidly evolving underwater technology market.
