Return Policy

We take pride in our goods' quality and want you to be entirely delighted with your purchase. If you are unhappy with your purchase, we will gladly issue a complete refund or exchange within 14 days of purchase.

Please send us an email at support@playtosy.com with your order number and reason for return to begin a return. Within 24 hours, our customer care staff will contact you with information on how to return the item. Please keep in mind that the item must be returned in its original condition and packing, and it must be accompanied by valid proof of purchase.

We will handle your refund or exchange within 3 business days of receiving your return and verifying that the item is in its original condition. Refunds will be made in the same method of payment that was used to make the original transaction. Please keep in mind that shipping and handling expenses are not refundable, and customers are responsible for return shipping charges.

Please notify us if you get a faulty or broken item, and we will work with you to rectify the issue as soon as possible. We may need images of the damaged item to assist us in identifying the problem and expediting the resolution process.

Please keep in mind that certain things, such as customized or custom-made items, are not returnable unless they are faulty or damaged.
