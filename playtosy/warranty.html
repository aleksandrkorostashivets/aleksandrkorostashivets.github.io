Warranty Info

We take pleasure in the quality of our items at Playtosy.com, and we want our customers to be confident in their purchases. Here are some crucial details regarding our warranty:

From the date of purchase, all of our items are covered by a 2-year guarantee that covers any faults in materials or manufacture.

Contact us right away at support@playtosy.com if you get a faulty or damaged item. We will try with you to remedy the issue as soon as possible, and we may need images of the damaged item to assist us in identifying the issue.

Please contact us for a refund or replacement if your item stops operating or becomes useless during the warranty term.

Normal wear and use, damage resulting from accidents or misuse, and any changes made to the goods are not covered by our guarantee.

Please contact our customer service staff at support@playtosy.com if you have any questions or complaints regarding our warranty policy. We're always willing to assist.
