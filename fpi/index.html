  • FPSO Project

    • Aasta Hansteen Spar
      • Type: SPAR
      • Status: Installed
      • Country: Norway
      • Field: Aasta Hansteen
      • Field Operator: Equinor
      • Unit Owner: Equinor

      Technip/Hyundai had the $660 mil EPCI contract for the spar hull and mooring system. The 200 X 50 m structure is the largest spar hull to date and the first spar designed for storage. Hyundai had a separate $1.1 bil EPCI contract for the topsides. The integrated deck, living quarters and flare has a dry weight of 21,500 tonnes. Hertel Offshore provided the accommodation module for $90 mil. Aker Solutions did the hull FEED. CB&I did the topsides FEED and had a $180 mil engineering/procurement subcontract from Hyundai.

    • Abadi
      • Type: FPSO
      • Status: Planned
      • Country: Indonesia
      • Field: Masela
      • Field Operator: Inpex
      • Development Stage: Pre-FEED but stalled due to climate issues

      The development plan for Abadi focuses on a newly-built 9.5 mtpa capacity land LNG plant in South Tanimbar Indonesia serviced by an FPSO moored 180 km offshore to strip liquids and pipe gas to shore. The FPSO will have capability to process 1.8 Bcf/d of gas and 36,000 b/d of condensate.

    • Abigail-Joseph FPSO
      • Type: FPSO
      • Status: Installed
      • Country: Nigeria
      • Field: OML 83/85
      • Field Operator: First E&P
      • Unit Owner: Yinson

      First E&P in Feb 2019 contracted with Yinson to supply an FPSO for use on the Anyala and Madu fields offshore Nigeria. FPSO Allan was modified at Keppel for this contract. Keppel's work scope encompassed refurbishment and life extension work, fabrication and installation of a new riser balcony, spread mooring system and helideck, and modifications to the FPSO's topsides and marine systems. Work began in Q3 2019, delivery was in Feb 2020 and production start was in Nov 2020
