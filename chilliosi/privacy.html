privacy policy

Chilliciosi.com greets you! We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. We also take our spices seriously, but that's another story.

Here's what you should know:

The Data We Gather

When you visit our website or make a purchase, we may collect personal information from you, such as your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and preferences. Non-personal information such as browser type, operating system, and IP address may also be collected.

How We Make Use of Your Data

Your personal information may be used to process orders, provide customer service, and personalize your shopping experience. We may also use your information for marketing purposes, but we will not bombard you with emails. You have the option to opt out at any time.

Sharing Your Knowledge

Third-party service providers such as shipping companies, payment processors, and marketing agencies may receive your personal information. Your information may also be disclosed if required by law or in response to a court order.

Security

We are concerned about the security of your personal information and use industry-standard encryption to protect it during transmission. We also maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to prevent unauthorized access to your information.

Cookies

Cookies are used to enhance your shopping experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer that allow us to remember your preferences and recognize you on subsequent visits.

Third-Party Connections

Links to third-party websites may appear on our website. We are not responsible for these websites' privacy practices or content.

Your Legal Rights

You have the right to access and update your personal information, as well as to opt out of receiving marketing communications.
