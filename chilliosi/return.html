return policy

We take great pride in our product quality and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are dissatisfied with your order for any reason, please contact us within 14 days of receiving your shipment and we will gladly assist you with a return or exchange.

Your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it to be eligible for a return or exchange. It also has to be in its original packaging. A receipt or proof of purchase is required to complete your return.

Please keep in mind that shipping costs for returns are non-refundable, and the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Because we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item, we recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance.

We will send you an email once your return has been received and inspected to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you whether your refund has been approved or rejected.

If you are approved, your refund will be processed and a credit will be applied to your original method of payment within a certain number of days.
