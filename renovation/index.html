Our company provides you with a wide range of services

Whether you are starting to build a new house, extend the house, do renovation… We will take the time to ensure that you get maximum satisfaction from our work.

Our company specializes in concrete work, basement waterproofing, brick and stone renovation/installation, tile works, flagstones works, basement staircase or ramp, concrete works, decorative concrete, stamped and/or color concrete, hardwood/vinyl/laminate flooring installation, drywall installation, demolition handyman work installing and/or replacing windows and doors and more…

Also, if you’re not sure what exactly you need for your renovation, we’ll help you with ideas, color of materials, type of material, budget, design needs, and more. Our team will make sure that the work will be done well on time and that you are satisfied with the work.
