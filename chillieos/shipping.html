Shipping info

Chillieos.com is dedicated to assisting you in making the most of your outdoor cooking experience. We understand that once you've decided to purchase a high-quality grill, you'll want to use it right away. That is why we work hard to get your order to you as soon as possible.

When you place an order with us, we will make every effort to process and ship it within 1-2 business days. If you have any questions about your shipment or need to make changes to your order, please contact our helpful customer service team. We're always willing to assist.
