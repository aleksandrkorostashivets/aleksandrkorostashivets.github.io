Warranty info

Chillieos.com guarantees the quality of our outdoor grills. We are confident that you will enjoy your purchase, but in the unlikely event that something goes wrong, we have you covered.

Our grills are all backed by a sizzling warranty that covers any defects in materials and workmanship. If you have any problems with your grill, please let us know and we will do our best to resolve the problem.

Please keep in mind that our warranty excludes damage caused by misuse or normal wear and tear. However, if you have any questions about how to care for your grill, we're always here to help.

We want you to have the best grilling experience possible, so please contact us if you have any questions about your purchase or the warranty. We'll do everything in our power to make things right.
