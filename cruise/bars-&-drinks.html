Vibrant Bars & Exquisite Drinks

Relax and unwind at our wide selection of bars, where expertly crafted cocktails and an extensive drink menu await you. Whether you prefer a classic cocktail, a glass of fine wine, or something unique created by our talented bartenders, you’ll find the perfect spot to enjoy your favorite beverage.

Each bar onboard offers a different atmosphere, from the lively poolside bar to the elegant cocktail lounge. Sip your drink while gazing at the sunset, or join in on the evening’s entertainment with live music and a vibrant crowd. The options are endless, and each one promises an unforgettable experience.

Our bartenders are passionate about their craft, constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to bring you something extraordinary. If you have a favorite drink in mind, they are always ready to tailor it to your liking.

Whether you're socializing with fellow passengers or enjoying a quiet evening, our bars provide the perfect setting. Come and experience the best in mixology, paired with the stunning backdrop of the open sea.

Oceanview Lounge

The Oceanview Lounge offers a serene escape, where you can enjoy expertly crafted cocktails while gazing at the open sea. This bar is perfect for a quiet afternoon drink or a romantic evening with a loved one. The lounge specializes in classic cocktails like the Old Fashioned, Martini, and Negroni, all made with premium spirits and fresh ingredients.

In addition to cocktails, the Oceanview Lounge features a wide selection of wines, ranging from crisp whites to full-bodied reds. Enjoy a glass of wine or a signature cocktail while relaxing in a comfortable setting, surrounded by the sound of the waves and a gentle ocean breeze. The bar's atmosphere is designed to provide an elegant yet casual space for guests to unwind.

Poolside Bar

For those looking for a fun and vibrant atmosphere, the Poolside Bar is the place to be. Serving refreshing frozen drinks, tropical cocktails, and ice-cold beers, this bar is ideal for cooling off after a swim or sunbathing by the pool. Popular drinks include the piña colada, margarita, and daiquiri, all blended to perfection.

The Poolside Bar also offers light snacks, making it easy to grab a quick bite while enjoying the sunshine. Whether you're sipping on a mojito or enjoying a local craft beer, the bar’s lively setting and upbeat music create a vacation vibe that's perfect for a day of relaxation.

Whiskey & Cigar Lounge

Indulge in a sophisticated evening at our Whiskey & Cigar Lounge, where you can enjoy a fine selection of single malt whiskeys, bourbons, and cigars from around the world. This lounge is the perfect setting for those who appreciate the finer things in life, offering an atmosphere of old-world elegance with leather chairs, dark wood furnishings, and dim lighting.

Choose from an extensive whiskey list, featuring rare and aged bottles from Scotland, Ireland, Japan, and the United States. Pair your drink with a hand-rolled cigar from our curated selection, and enjoy the relaxed and refined ambiance. The lounge also offers whiskey flights for those looking to sample and explore different varieties.
