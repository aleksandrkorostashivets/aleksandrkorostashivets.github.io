A service leaf guards installation typically includes:

  • Assessment

    An initial assessment of the property to determine the most suitable type of leaf guard system based on factors like roof type, foliage density, and local climate conditions.

  • Measurement and Customization

    Taking precise measurements of the gutters and downspouts to ensure a proper fit, and customizing the leaf guard materials accordingly.

  • Preparation

    Clearing debris from the gutters and downspouts, ensuring they are clean and free of any obstructions before installing the leaf guards.

  • Installation

    Installing the leaf guard system along the gutters and downspouts, securely attaching it to the roofline and ensuring proper alignment to effectively divert water and prevent debris buildup.

  • Sealing and Fastening

    Sealing any seams or joints in the leaf guard system to prevent leaks, and securely fastening it to the gutter system to withstand various weather conditions.

  • Testing

    Conducting tests to ensure that the leaf guard system is functioning correctly, allowing water to flow freely while blocking debris effectively.
