About Us

Marine News is the premier magazine serving North America’s shallow-draft workboat markets—towboats, tugs & barges, dredging, patrol & multi-mission craft—delivering the largest printed audience in the segment with targeted bonus distribution at key events.

Marine News + MarineLink.com — the magazine, the website, the eNewsletter, the Podcast, the TV channel, and our social media platforms — together form the maritime industry’s largest and most diverse media family. This integrated approach ensures your message reaches maritime professionals wherever they consume content.

As the flagship of the maritime sector’s largest network of print and electronic media, Marine News reaches the global industry’s highest combined business circulation. We deliver comprehensive analysis, breaking news, and essential market intelligence to shipowners, operators, engineers, port authorities, offshore energy companies, and maritime technology providers worldwide.

We deliver the most compelling, cost-efficient, and effective media buy in the maritime industry, connecting your brand with the decision-makers who matter most. Our advertisers leverage our unparalleled reach, sterling reputation, and deep industry expertise to maximize their impact in this specialized market.

From cutting-edge vessel technology and regulatory updates to market trends and executive insights, Marine News remains the essential resource for maritime professionals navigating an increasingly complex global industry.
