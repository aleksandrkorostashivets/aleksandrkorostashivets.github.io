return policy

Chilifie.com is a place where spice is our middle name! We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase, but we understand that things don't always go as planned. That is why we provide a simple and hassle-free return policy.

If you are dissatisfied with your order for any reason, please contact us within 30 days of receipt to begin the return process. We will provide you with a return shipping label and issue a full refund once we receive the returned item in its original condition.

Due to health and safety concerns, we are unable to accept returns of opened food items.

Thank you for choosing Chilifie.com, and we look forward to adding some spice to your life again soon!
