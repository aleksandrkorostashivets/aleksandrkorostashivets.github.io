Privacy policy

Chilifie.com is your yet another shop for all things spicy! We value your privacy and want you to have a pleasant shopping experience while remaining confident that your personal information is being handled appropriately.

We may gather identifiable data from you when you visit our website, such as your name, email address, and billing information. Cookies and similar technologies are also used to track your activity on our site and to personalize your experience.

We use your personal information to process orders, communicate with you about our products and services, and to customize your experience on our website. Your information may also be used for marketing purposes, such as sending you promotional offers or newsletters.

Information Sharing: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may, however, share your information with service providers who help us fulfill orders or with analytics providers who help us understand how our website is used.

Your Options: At any time, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also unsubscribe from our marketing communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in any of our emails.

Security: We take reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your personal information. However, no security system is perfect, and we cannot guarantee the safety of your data.

Changes to this Policy: From time to time, we may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy on a regular basis to stay up to date on our data practices.
