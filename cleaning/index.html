/About us

01 Does the same cleaner come to the same location?

Yes, we strive to provide consistency by assigning the same cleaner to your location whenever possible. This allows our cleaners to become familiar with your specific preferences and needs, ensuring a more personalized and thorough cleaning experience each time.

02 What do we not clean?

For health and safety reasons, we do not clean biohazards, such as mold, blood, or other bodily fluids. Additionally, we avoid cleaning areas with excessive clutter or personal items, and we do not handle heavy lifting or moving of furniture. Our focus is on providing a safe and high-quality cleaning service within these guidelines.

03 Can I request eco-friendly cleaning products?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of using environmentally friendly products for the safety of your family and pets. Upon request, our team can use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are just as effective in maintaining a clean and healthy home.

04 Do you offer a pet-friendly cleaning service?

Yes, we offer a pet-friendly cleaning service tailored to homes with furry friends. Our team uses pet-safe cleaning products and takes extra care to ensure that your pets are comfortable during the cleaning process. We also pay special attention to pet areas, removing fur, dander, and any messes, so your home is clean and fresh for both you and your pets.

A team you can count on

Little things matter. Our cleaners pay close attention to every detail, ensuring no spot is missed and your space looks immaculate after every visit.

Skilled and certified

Our cleaners undergo rigorous training and certification to ensure they are equipped with the latest techniques and best practices in the industry. You can trust our team to handle even the toughest cleaning challenges.

Reliable and trustworthy

Letting someone into your home or workplace requires trust. All our team members are thoroughly vetted, background-checked, and insured, giving you peace of mind every time we clean.

Friendly and professional

Our team is not only skilled but also approachable and respectful. We believe in providing excellent customer service with a smile, making your experience with us pleasant and hassle-free.
