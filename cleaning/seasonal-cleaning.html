FAQ

  • 01

    What services are included in seasonal cleaning?

    Our seasonal cleaning service includes a thorough deep cleaning of all rooms, window and mirror washing, carpet and upholstery cleaning, dusting hard-to-reach areas, comprehensive kitchen and bathroom cleaning, and balcony and terrace cleaning. We can also accommodate additional requests upon your specification.

  • 02

    How often should I get seasonal cleaning done?

    It is recommended to perform seasonal cleaning twice a year: in the spring and in the fall. Spring cleaning helps to remove accumulated winter dust and dirt, while fall cleaning prepares your home for the winter months, creating a cozy and clean environment.

  • 03

    How long does seasonal cleaning take?

    The duration of seasonal cleaning depends on the size of your home and the scope of work required. Typically, cleaning takes between 4 to 8 hours. Our professionals can provide a more accurate estimate after assessing your space.

  • 04

    Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies and equipment?

    No, our team will bring all the necessary professional cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you have any preferred products or equipment, you are welcome to provide them, and we will be happy to use them.
