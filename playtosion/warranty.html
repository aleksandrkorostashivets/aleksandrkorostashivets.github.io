Warranty info

When you buy one of our laser tag weapons from Playtosion.com, we want you to have complete peace of mind. As a result, we provide a limited warranty on all of our products.

We will replace your laser tag weapon if it becomes defective due to manufacturer's defects within 12 months of purchase. Simply contact us at warranty@playtosion.com and we will send you instructions on how to return your weapon for repair or replacement.

We must emphasize that our warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, neglect, or accidental damage, as well as normal wear and tear. If your weapon has been damaged and is no longer covered by our warranty, we may be able to repair it for a fee.

Don't hesitate to get in touch with us at warranty@playtosion.com if you have any questions or concerns about our warranty policy. We're always happy to assist and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Thank you for selecting Playtosion.com for all of your laser tag requirements!
