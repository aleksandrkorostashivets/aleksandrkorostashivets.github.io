Shipping info

Playtosion.com strives to deliver your order as quickly and efficiently as possible. What you need to know about our shipping policy is as follows:

We aim to process and ship all orders within 1-2 business days, and once your order has shipped, we'll send you an email with your tracking number. Shipping times may vary depending on where you live, but we will always use the most dependable and cost-effective shipping method for your order.

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and will be displayed before you complete your purchase at checkout. We ship using reputable carriers such as DHL and FedEx to ensure that your order arrives on time and in good condition.

If your order arrives damaged or not at all, please contact us immediately at shipping@playtosion.com and we will work with you to resolve the issue.

Playtosion.com is dedicated to providing you with the best shopping experience possible, which includes prompt and dependable shipping. Please contact us at shipping@playtosion.com if you have any questions or concerns about shipping.
