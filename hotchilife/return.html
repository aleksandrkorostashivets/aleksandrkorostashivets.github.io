return policy

We understand that sometimes things just don't work out. However, at HotChilife.com, we want to ensure that you are always happy with your purchases. So, if you're not feeling the heat, let us know and we'll do everything in our power to make it right.

Send us an email at returns@hotchilife.com with your order number and the reason for your return to get started. We'll get back to you as soon as you can say "jalapeo" with instructions on how to return your items.

Please keep in mind that we can only accept unused and undamaged items for return. We'll issue a refund to your original form of payment as soon as we receive your return as you can say "habanero."

Don't be concerned if your item arrives damaged or defective. Just let us know as soon as possible, and we'll make it right. We may even request photographs of the damage in order to improve our packaging and shipping methods.

Unless the item arrived damaged or defective, HotChilife.com is not responsible for any shipping fees associated with returns. While we will do our best to process your return as soon as possible, please allow up to two weeks for your refund to appear in your account.

Thank you for using HotChilife.com to spice up your life! We hope our products ignite your passion, but if they don't, we've got your back.
