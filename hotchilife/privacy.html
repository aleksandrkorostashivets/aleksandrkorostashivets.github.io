Certain personal information may be passively collected when you visit the Website, which means it is gathered without your actively providing it. Your IP address, location (city/state/country), website usage information, and device information may all be included in this data.

On the Website, we use cookies to improve the quality of our Website and services to you. We use cookies for a variety of purposes in order to provide a fast and secure digital experience, such as maintaining an active connection while navigating through the Website pages, storing credentials in a secure mode, and identifying previously visited Website pages in order to prevent them from being proposed again.

A cookie is a small text file that our Website sends to your computer to store information that uniquely identifies our Users. Cookies used on the Website store a token that uniquely identifies a specific user at a specific time. This token is then associated with the user data in our secure databases. You can always delete the cookies and configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to display a message when one is sent. If you reset your browser, some of the Website's features and functionality may be unavailable to you, and you may not be able to use all of the Website's features.
